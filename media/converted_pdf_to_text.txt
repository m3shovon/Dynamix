Project Plan: Gas Station ERP/POS System
Requirements Necessary for the SAAS:
Payment Gateways:
●
Integrate with payment processors to handle transactions directly through the system.
Subscription module:
●
Without designing the proper subscription model we can’t handle the automated
registration process.
Mobile Responsiveness:
●
Develop a mobile responsive system.
Flexible Shift Configurations:
●
Allow customization of shift durations, number of daily shifts, and shift timings to
accommodate operational needs.
●
To add shifts to a particular employee, a small HRM portion is needed.
Overlapping Shifts Handling:
●
Prevent conflicts by managing overlapping shift schedules.
